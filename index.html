Diamond Nation Studios

By entering a partnership with Diamond Nation Studios, your server agrees to meet and maintain the eligibility requirement of having at least fifteen members, unless an exception is formally approved by the Founders of Diamond Nation Studios.

Upon acceptance, you are required to fully comply with all branding standards set by DNS, which includes the mandatory use of all provided logos, decals, and promotional materials exactly as delivered, without alteration or redistribution of any kind.

Additionally, your server must establish a role titled "Diamond Nation," designated as the highest administrative role in your server, equipped with full administrative permissions to ensure proper oversight and coordination with DNS leadership.

All logos, decals, and branding assets remain the exclusive property of Diamond Nation Studios and are only licensed for use during the active duration of the partnership. Misuse of assets, failure to uphold branding requirements, or refusal to maintain proper administrative structure may result in immediate removal from the program.

If the partnership is terminated by either party, you are required to remove, retract, and replace every DNS-provided decal, logo, and visual asset from your server, game, documents, and any associated materials within 48 hours.

By participating in the Diamond Nation Studios network, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to all terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement and that you accept full responsibility for maintaining compliance throughout the duration of the partnership.
